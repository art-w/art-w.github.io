subst b ~subst s, using b, substitutes in s variables of the form "$(doc)" by their subst definition. This leaves escapes and markup directives $(markup,â€¦) intact.

doc_to_plain b ~subst s using b, substitutes in s variables by their subst definition and renders cmdliner directives to plain text.
